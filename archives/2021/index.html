Biography

I’m Yiming Dou (窦铱明), an undergraduate from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), majoring in Computer Science and Technology and minor in Economics. I am a member of Zhiyuan Honors Program (Engineering). Currently, I work as an undergraduate researcher in Machine Vision and Intelligence Group (MVIG) since 2020.10, and now I’m doing research on computer vision under the guidance of Prof. Cewu Lu and Dr. Yong-Lu Li. My research interest lies in computer vision, multi-modal learning and robotics

Research Experience

Machine Vision and Intelligence Group (MVIG) (2020.10 ~ Present)

  • Learned basic skills of computer vision (e.g. PyTorch, Stanford CS231n courses)
  • Built a dataset based on AVA by annotating Human Body Part States (PaSta) and 3D location‑size of interactive objects.
  • Designed a semantic space for activity class settings under the situation of vision‑language (VL) joint learning.
  • Built a fitting engine: POSE_to_SMPL for 3D pose alignment that unifies all kinds of 3D pose datasets into SMPL parameters.
  • Implemented a code base that generates synthetic data from VIBE or ROMP results.
  • Performed very detailed prompt learning experiments, thus improving performance of a vision‑language joint learning model
